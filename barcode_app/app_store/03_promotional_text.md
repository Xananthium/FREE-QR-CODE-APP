## A/B Testing Strategy

Every 2 weeks, rotate between:
1. **Feature-focused** (what it does)
2. **Story-driven** (why it exists)
3. **Privacy-first** (what it doesn't do)

Track downloads to see which resonates most with your audience.

---

## Writing Tips for Promotional Text

✓ **DO:**
- Start with a benefit or emotional hook
- Use short, punchy sentences
- Include 2-3 key differentiators
- End with brand or mission
- Update regularly (every 2-4 weeks)

✗ **DON'T:**
- Use vague marketing speak
- Waste characters on filler words
- Make claims you can't back up
- Forget to mention "free"
- Leave it unchanged for months
