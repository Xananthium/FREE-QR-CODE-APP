## The Description

**Never Pay to Keep Your QR Codes Working. Ever.**

Create unlimited QR codes that work forever—completely free. No hidden costs, no expiring links, no ransom emails asking for money to keep your codes active.

**Why This App Exists**

It was my daughter's sixth birthday party and I thought it would be cute to put a QR code on the flyers with the Google map link. I went to a website that said it was free. Great, made it, put it on the flyer. Three days later I got an email saying that if I wanted the code to stay active, I needed to buy a yearly membership for $30. Needless to say I did not buy the membership and I got a bunch of calls saying the QR code didn't work. Never again.

That's why we built this app. Your QR codes should work forever, without anyone holding them hostage.

**What You Can Create**

• **URLs & Websites** - Share links that never expire
• **Contact Cards** - Let people save your info instantly (vCard)
• **WiFi Credentials** - Share your network without typing passwords
• **Plain Text** - Any message, note, or information
• **Email & Phone** - One-tap communication links
• **SMS Messages** - Pre-filled text messages
• **Calendar Events** - Add events with one scan
• **Locations** - Geographic coordinates for maps

**Features That Matter**

✓ **Truly Free Forever** - No trials, no subscriptions, no "premium" upsells
✓ **Works Offline** - Generate codes without internet connection
✓ **No Expiration** - Your codes work forever, guaranteed
✓ **Instant Sharing** - Save, print, or share immediately
✓ **Multiple Formats** - Save as PNG, JPEG, or SVG
✓ **Customizable** - Choose size, color, and error correction level
✓ **Batch Creation** - Generate multiple codes quickly
✓ **History** - Access previously created codes anytime
