## Crisis Management

### If Something Goes Wrong

**Bug Discovered:**
1. Acknowledge immediately
2. Provide timeline for fix
3. Update when fixed
4. Transparency wins trust

**Privacy Concern Raised:**
1. Address head-on (never defensive)
2. Provide technical details
3. If valid, fix immediately
4. Thank the reporter publicly

**Negative Review Goes Viral:**
1. Respond professionally
2. Offer to resolve offline
3. Show you care
4. Turn critics into advocates
