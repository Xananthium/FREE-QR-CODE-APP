## Keyword Research Notes

**What users search for:**
1. "free qr code generator" - HIGH volume
2. "qr code maker" - MEDIUM volume
3. "offline qr code" - LOW volume (but our strength)
4. "no ads qr" - LOW volume (but our differentiator)
5. "永久二维码" - MEDIUM volume (Chinese market)

**Competitor keywords to avoid:**
- "scan" alone (we're primarily a generator)
- "reader" alone (not our main function)
- "dynamic" (we don't do dynamic/trackable codes)

**Long-tail opportunities:**
- Users searching for "free without subscription"
- Users burned by ransom tactics
- Privacy-conscious users

## Tips for App Store Optimization

1. **Name + Subtitle** should contain your top 3 keywords
2. **Keywords field** should NOT repeat words from name/subtitle
3. **Use commas** to separate (spaces are wasted characters)
4. **No plural** forms needed (Apple indexes both)
5. **Test variations** every update cycle
