## Design Guidelines

### Typography
- **Headlines:** Bold, 32-40pt, high contrast
- **Body:** Regular, 18-24pt, readable
- **Accent:** Light/Medium, 14-18pt

### Colors
- **Background:** Clean white or very light gray
- **Text:** Dark gray/black for readability
- **Accents:** Your app's brand color (suggest: honest blue or trustworthy green)
- **Checkmarks:** Green (#00C853)
- **X marks:** Red (#F44336)

### Layout
- Leave 15% margin on all sides
- Text should not cover critical UI elements
- Ensure text is readable at thumbnail size
- Use consistent positioning across all screenshots

### Mood
- **Professional** but approachable
- **Trustworthy** (this is key given the scam story)
- **Clean** and uncluttered
- **Confident** without being aggressive
