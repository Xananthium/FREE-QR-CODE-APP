A free, open-source QR code generator that will never charge subscription fees. Born from getting scammed by a "free" QR code service during a 6-year-old's birthday party planning.

**Platforms**:
- Android (APK ready for distribution)
- iOS (ready for App Store submission)
- Web (single HTML file, works offline)

## üéØ Project Goals

1. **Free Forever** - No subscriptions, no ads, no tricks
2. **Privacy First** - Works 100% offline, no tracking
3. **Beautiful Design** - Museum-quality aesthetic with premium typography
4. **Easy to Use** - Swipe between QR types, live preview, one-tap export
5. **Open Source** - MIT licensed, encourage contributions
